About Communications Lab!

This course is an introductory level course that is open to students interested in interactive media, regardless of their prior experience or knowledge! It's main goal is to introduce students to the most basic programs and platforms that can be used in making a website and helping them understand the fundamental elements of interactive media.

With assignments, students were able to look into different elements of media such as sound and video. We learned how to record good sound by using adequate recorders etc. and editing the audio files so that it best meets the audience's expectation. We also learned how to use cameras and edit the videos for them to best suite the websites we are creating.

After the whole curriculum of this course, I think I learned most of the basic parts of what makes a product an interactive media. It may not be the most professional, but we know how to implement things that we can draw in our heads. This will be the start of our journey to mastering interactive media.

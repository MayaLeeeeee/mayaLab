Project #1

30 Minutes Film Festival

In this project, students were given 30 minutes to create a film. Everything- from brainstorming the ideas to filming and editing- was done in just 30 minutes.

This term's 30 Minutes Film Festival (30MFF) theme was "CONNECTION" and "INTERACTION." When we thought about connection, we naturally agreed that connection, interaction, and communication these days, especially in the midst of Covid-19, is done through our electronic or mobile devices and internet.

With this idea, we thought about how when we connect our chargers to the socket and turn our phones on, we are able to connect with anyone, wherever they are. We emphasized this point by reversing the video once, implying that as chargers come off and phones are turned off, connection is lost or becomes harder.

The film was taken with classmates Yasmin Alshorafa and Tina Zhang. The website was done as an individual project.

Project #2

The Missing Cat

The second project was about creating a comic and base it and display it on the web. Our team thought hard about how to create an original content. As we were not originally comic creators, it was hard to come up with such topic. So, we decided to have our target audience as the New York University Abu Dhabi student body and create a content that all NYUAD students would be able to relate to.

That's when we came up with our campus cats! Our thought started from the imgination what if a cat got lost on our campus? As NYUAD students and campus cat lover ousrelves, we knew that this would be a big issue on campus and that everybody would join us on the solving the mystery.

So, that's where our story starts- the missing cat. Will you join us on the journey?

The project was done with classmates Eunsu Choi, Tina Zhang, and Chinonyerem Ukaegbu.

Project #3

CL Travel Agency

Hello! Welcome to CL Travel Agency!

We are sure that you are dying to get on a plane and travel soemwhere. Anywhere!

Covid-19 maken it hard to travel around different countries for all of us around the world. Don't worry! Our travel agency will give you a call to check your preferences. To make it easier for you to picture your travel destination. Close your eyes, make your choices.. and voila! It'll feel like you're there!

This project started from the thought that we want to have interactivyt and through that interactivity, it'd be nice if the sound could build up the more user interacts. We thought about portraying a landscape, but with sound. We agreed that the best concept to put out this functionality was to have a travel agency and allow the user to actually hear the sound of their travel destination.

This project was done with classmates Riley Ha, Cole Beasley, and Yasmin Alshorafa.

Project #4

How to Spend Time Safely at Home - Origami

This project started from our conversation that 'everyday gets so boring during the pandemic.' We were all tired of staying home. Even the games we used to like seemed more boring that just having a walk outside.

We wondered, 'how hard would it be to be a mother of a kid during this pandemic?' We thought that Covid-19 would be hard for kids and their parents as much as, if not more, it is for us.

With this thought, we decided that in this project, we want to introduce people to a new activity they can do safely at home. We wanted to have the target as everyone, from kids and parents to adults. We thought about an activity in which introductory level is easy for everybody to follow.

Then, we came up with ORIGAMI

Origami is a good activity for nearly everyone as there are works suitable for each level. Also, as for adults, it is a nostalgic activity they did as a kid. We believe this activity will warm your hear in these cold times.

So, are you bored? Follow along our origami tutorial videos and get yourself a new hobby!

This project was done with classmates Eunsu Choi, Nouf Alabbasi, and Yasmin Alshorafa.
